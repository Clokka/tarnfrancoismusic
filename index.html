About Tarn

Tarn Francois is a young guitarist from the UK with hundreds of gigs under his belt. Having performed in various bands across the country, Tarn is now focused on his debut studio album. His music blends blues, rock, and alternative styles, delivering soulful guitar work and raw energy. From intimate venues to festival stages, Tarn brings a dynamic presence and a deep love for live performance.
