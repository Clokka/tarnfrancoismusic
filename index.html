About Tarn

Tarn Francois is a young guitarist from the UK. Tarn has experience playing hundreds of gigs in various bands, and has recently been working on a brand new studio album. His music draws influence from rock, blues, and alternative genres. From small local venues to larger stages, Tarn delivers passionate, dynamic performances.
