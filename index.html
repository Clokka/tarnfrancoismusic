About Tarn

Tarn Francois is a dynamic young guitarist and songwriter from the UK, known for his bold fusion of classic blues, high-octane funk, and expressive rock. From festivals to street corners, Tarn brings raw energy, groove-heavy solos, and a vintage soul that feels both fresh and timeless. With hundreds of gigs behind him and many more to come, his sound is pure fire – funky, gritty, and unforgettable.
