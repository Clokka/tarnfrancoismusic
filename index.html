About Tarn

Tarn Francois is a genre-blending guitarist based in the UK. His music channels the raw emotion of blues with the grit of alternative rock — all wrapped in expressive, soulful performances. After years of live gigs and collaborations, Tarn is now focused on his debut studio album.
